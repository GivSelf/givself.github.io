Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

GivSelf is a self-hosted application. All data processing happens on your own server and devices. We do not operate any cloud services, collect any data, or have access to your information.

Data Collection

The GivSelf app and server do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics services, crash reporting, or tracking pixels.
  • No accounts — GivSelf does not require user accounts or registration.
  • No cloud storage — All energy data, settings, and credentials are stored locally on your self-hosted server's database.

Data Stored on Your Server

The GivSelf server, which you host and control, stores:

  • Energy metrics from your inverter (power readings, energy totals)
  • Configuration settings (inverter connection details, API keys you provide)
  • Solar forecast data from third-party services you choose to enable (Solcast, Forecast.Solar)
  • Historical energy flow data imported from the GivEnergy Cloud API

This data never leaves your server unless you explicitly configure third-party integrations.

Third-Party Services

GivSelf can optionally connect to the following services if you provide API credentials:

  • GivEnergy Cloud API — To fetch historical energy data and system information. Subject to GivEnergy's terms.
  • Solcast — For solar generation forecasts. Subject to Solcast's privacy policy.
  • Forecast.Solar — For solar generation estimates. Your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to their API. Subject to Forecast.Solar's privacy policy.

Mobile App

The GivSelf mobile app connects only to your self-hosted server. The server URL is stored locally on your device. No data is sent to GivSelf developers or any other party.

Local Network Access

The app requires local network access to communicate with your GivSelf server. This permission is used solely for connecting to the server you configure in the app's settings.

Children's Privacy

GivSelf is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. Since GivSelf is open-source, you can always review the source code to verify our privacy practices.

Contact
